Bentonite

Introduction

Bentonite is a clay rock, also known as montmorillonite clay rock, normally containing a small amount of illite, kaolin, halloysite, chlorite, zeolite, quartz, feldspar, calcite and the like; hardness 1-2, density 2~ 3g / cm3. Exchangeable cations of the smectite type, content and layer charge size, bentonite can be divided sodium bentonite (alkaline soil), calcium bentonite (alkaline soil), natural bleaching earth (soil acidity or acidic clay), which calcium include calcium bentonite and sodium and calcium and magnesium group.
