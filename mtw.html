Working principle

The qualified materials after crushing are uniformly and quantitatively fed into the main machine grinding chamber through the variable frequency belt feeder for grinding. The material after grinding is sent to the classifier by the fan airflow for classification. Under the action of the analyzer, the materials that do not meet the fineness requirements fall into the grinding chamber and are re-milled. The qualified materials enter the cyclone collector with the airflow through the pipeline.
